CORPORATE WALK (MARCHE)

  • Each team can have 5 to 8 members on stage
  • Each team will have 10 minutes to put up the show with a 1-minute stage set up. Negative points will be awarded for exceeding the time limit.
  • The theme should be strictly related to workplace attire.
  • Background music to be in an mp3 format in CD/pen drive and need to be submitted to the event heads 1 hour prior to the event. Technical issues and format compatibility for the audio files will be the sole responsibility of the team.
  • Teams will be judged based on the costume, theme, walking, stance and attitude.
  • Use of cigarettes, alcohol and any unfair means is strongly prohibited both on and off stage.
  • The vulgarity of any kind is prohibited.
  • Green room facilities will be provided
  • If a participant is found violating any of these rules, it would lead to the disqualification of the participant
  • Rules listed are subject to change as per the discretion of the Event Heads and the Organizing Committee.
